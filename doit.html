Try it out: Click Here to Try it

doIt, created in 2023, was created to help my become more proficient in JavaScript, specifically with React.js in preparation for larger future projects. This hands-on experience allowed me to explore the framework's capabilities and best practices.

The application utilizes local storage to manage user lists, ensuring data persistence on the user's machine. Additionally, it offers various sorting options for the items to enhance usability and functionality.

This project not only helped get me acquanted with React.js but strengthened my JavaScript skills, providing a solid foundation for tackling more complex applications in the future.
